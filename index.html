About Us

State of Art is an initiative taken by the College Union in the effort to foster a college driven by creativity that provides the opportunity for everyone to engage in diverse arts and culture. We believe that great artisanship inspires us, brings us together and teaches about ourselves and the world around us.


The primary vision of the gallery is to procure, propose & promote an exclusive range of Contemporary Art, Modern Art and all other genres of Painting by exploring and evaluating artists. This online gallery showcases some of the finest collections of arts, crafts and painting of our eminent art experts.

Elevating our artists to new heights of glory

Our artistic community associate themselves with a circle of creativity. With numerous artists and artworks on display, State of Art has the companionship of multitudinous artworks by various artists.

We aim to provide a useful platform where the artists can publish and sell their artworks. As new waves of exciting young artists add new contours onto art, our team is on hand to support these sprouting talents in every step of the way

Peruse and Purchase Art online

State of Art expose a wide array of Contemporary & Modern Art & Paintings Online. Every piece of artwork displayed on the website is chosen by our crew to be put on site along with the artiste’s profile. These diverse pieces of artwork are sure to stick out and catch the eyes of the insightful art collector or even a first-time art buyer. The paintings are of different mediums like – Oil Paintings, Acrylic Paintings, Watercolour Paintings, Graphite and Charcoal sketches. State of Art showcases artworks on various style & subjects like – Abstract Paintings, Landscape Paintings, Portrait Paintings, Caricature works, Mandala art forms etc.

How to purchase your art?

State of Art has been made a very user-friendly site where the buyer can have a detailed resume of a specific Artist and choose your artwork which you find appealing. If you’re interested in a for-sale work, simply visit the artwork page and click on the Purchase Art Online option. You can further enquire about pricing, availability, shipping, and further details.
